ABOUT US

We are a dedicated team at IIT Goa, committed to advancing the field of high-voltage engineering. Our recent focus areas include understanding the degradation of polymeric insulators in high voltage transmission systems at higher altitudes, exploring the performance of nanocomposite dielectrics and developing superhydrophobic surfaces for outdoor insulation, applying digital image processing to study corona discharges, analyzing lightning electromagnetic fields and their effects, investigating surface charging of polymeric insulators, and advancing plasma applications. We're also devoted to using electrostatics for e-waste segregation.
