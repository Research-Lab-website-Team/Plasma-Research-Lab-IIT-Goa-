Biography

  • Biography Shakthi Prasad D. (Senior Member, IEEE) was born in Karnataka, India, in 1989. He received the B.E. degree in electrical and electronics engineering from the Malnad College of Engineering, Hassan, India, in 2011, the M.Tech. (Research) degree in power and energy systems from the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal, India, in 2013, and the Ph.D. degree from the Indian Institute of Science Bengaluru, Bengaluru, India, in 2018. He is currently an Assistant Professor with the School of Electrical Science, Indian Institute of Technology Goa, Goa, India. His research interests include the polymeric insulators for outdoor applications, high voltage engineering, electromagnetics, numerical techniques in electrostatics, and lightning induced disturbances in power system.
